About

• About us

It all started with two cat lovers who saw an opportunity and a necessity in the market to ﬁnd trusted hotels for our beloved babies, where we could be at ease knowing they would be taken good care of.

So we made our own.

We have meticulously researched, designed, sought advice and views from vets, cat owners and animal welfare organisations to ensure we offer a fantastic, unique, premium service for your valued family member.

• The hotel

Gourmet menus and grooming services, partners whose sole job it is to come in and entertain residents and accommodate the ones that need special needs. This meticulous attention to detail is what deﬁnes a stay at our nursery.

Every aspect of the hotel has been crafted around each individual cat’s well-being and the owner’s peace of mind.
